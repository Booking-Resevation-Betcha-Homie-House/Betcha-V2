About us

Feel at home, every stay.

What started as a single room and a big dream has grown into a cozy, affordable staycation experience for both travelers and locals. After the pandemic, in 2023, we were inspired to create a space where people could reconnect, recharge, and feel right at home.

Our name, Betcha, is a heartfelt tribute to the owner’s beloved mother — a symbol of warmth and hospitality that we pour into every guest’s experience.

Thanks to the love and trust of our guests, we've grown from that one humble room into a collection of thoughtfully designed spaces. Each one is crafted with care, reflecting our commitment to comfort, convenience, and personalized service.

Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat, a fun family escape, or a spot to work and unwind — we’re here to make your stay unforgettable.

Wondering why guests love staying here?

Hassle-free bookings

Booking your stay is quick and easy with our smooth reservation system. No confusing steps—just pick, click, and confirm.

Comfy rooms

Our rooms are spotless, cozy, and designed to help you unwind. Whether it’s a short stay or a weekend escape, you’ll feel right at home.

Top notch amenities

Enjoy premium amenities like fast Wi-Fi, hot showers, and all the essentials. We’ve got the extras that turn a stay into an experience.
